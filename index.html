About Jack

Learn more about my journey, philosophy, and achievements in gymnastics

My Story

My gymnastics journey began at age 8 when I joined a local gymnastics club in Nairobi. From the very first time I stepped onto the mat, I was captivated by the combination of strength, flexibility, and artistry that gymnastics demands.

Over the years, I competed in numerous national and regional competitions, earning several medals and accolades. My competitive career taught me discipline, perseverance, and the importance of proper technique.

After retiring from competitive gymnastics, I transitioned into coaching, realizing my true passion was helping others discover the joy and benefits of this incredible sport. For the past 10 years, I've dedicated myself to developing training programs that are safe, effective, and enjoyable for students of all ages and skill levels.

My Mission

My mission is to make high-quality gymnastics training accessible to everyone in Nairobi and beyond. I believe that gymnastics isn't just for elite athletes - it's a fantastic way for anyone to build strength, improve flexibility, boost confidence, and have fun.

I'm committed to creating a supportive, inclusive environment where students feel empowered to push their limits while respecting their bodies and individual progress.

Training Programs

Structured programs designed for different skill levels and goals

Beginner Program

Perfect for those new to gymnastics or returning after a long break. This program focuses on building a strong foundation:

  • Basic body positions and alignment
  • Fundamental rolls and balances
  • Introduction to handstands and cartwheels
  • Basic strength and flexibility exercises
  • Safe falling and landing techniques

Duration: 8-12 weeks | Sessions: 2-3 times per week

Join Beginner Program

Intermediate Program

For those with basic skills looking to advance to more challenging moves:

  • Handstand variations and presses
  • Round-offs and back handsprings
  • Introduction to flips and twists
  • Intermediate strength and conditioning
  • Routine development and flow

Duration: 12-16 weeks | Sessions: 3-4 times per week

Join Intermediate Program

Advanced Program

For experienced gymnasts aiming for competition-level skills:

  • Advanced tumbling passes
  • Multiple flips and twists
  • Specialized apparatus training
  • Competition preparation and mental training
  • Advanced strength and flexibility

Duration: Ongoing | Sessions: 4-6 times per week

Join Advanced Program

Flexibility Program

Specialized program focused exclusively on improving flexibility:

  • Splits training (front and middle)
  • Back flexibility and bridges
  • Shoulder mobility and opening
  • Active flexibility development
  • Injury prevention through proper stretching

Duration: 6-8 weeks | Sessions: 2-3 times per week

Join Flexibility Program
